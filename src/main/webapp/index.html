Neighborhood Nature


Create your next favorite leisure walking/jogging route to check out nature in your area!

After you select your start/end point(s), click "Submit and Generate Route"! On the next page, type in whatever plants or animals you want to see! You can specify a limit to how many results you want for each feature(s)! The default is five choices per a feature. If you want to query a specific multi-word phrase, put it in quotation marks
Example query: robin, 2 clovers, and "california poppy"

After you submit your features, click on the markers on the map to select the points you want on your route, and we'll generate a map for you! The link on the bottom of the map allows you to open the route on Google Maps!
